Who We Are

SOFTWARESENI IS A TIGHTLY KNIT TEAM OF DESIGNERS, DEVELOPERS, QUALITY ASSURANCE, AND CUSTOMER SERVICE. OUR COMPANY HEADQUARTERED IN SYDNEY AUSTRALIA WITH A DEVELOPMENT TEAM IN THE EVER CHARMING CITY YOGYAKARTA, INDONESIA.

We Are Fun

While we are work tirelessly to deliver results for the client, we also have fun. A lot. We have a company provided lunch together on Monday, delicious bread for breakfast, ice cream like madman on Friday, frantic Mario Kart racing everyday (we even have a champion board), and every Thursday there is a regular futsal match. There is a free movie monthly, and every 6 month we have a company outing filled with creative activities.

We Are World Wide

SoftwareSeni lives and works at the far ends of the world but our clients include brands and companies from Sydney, Melbourne, Auckland, Amsterdam, Los Angeles, New York and further afield. We care about the way we deliver our products but also the way we communicate with our clients.

We Are Young & Eager

We are proud to have our main development centre in Yogyakarta, a university city in Indonesia, and enjoy its culture, but we also take our craft seriously. We undertake comprehensive training with all our staff to develop skills and careers. We also have a commitment to supporting start-up businesses and encourage our staff to get involved.
